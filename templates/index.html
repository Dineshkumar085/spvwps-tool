Calculation Chain:
ET₀ = FAO-56 PM Eq.6 = [0.408·Δ·Rn + γ·(900/T+273)·u₂·(es–ea)] / [Δ + γ·(1+0.34·u₂)]
ETc = Kc × ET₀ (mm/day) → Kc adjusted per FAO-56 Eq.62 for local RHmin, wind
CWR = ETc × Days × Area × 10 (m³, since 1mm over 1ha = 10 m³)
Pe = Effective Rainfall = Total Rain × 0.70 (USDA-SCS; bounded by ETc)
NIR = max(CWR – Pe, 0) → GIR = NIR ÷ Field Application Efficiency
